What is an NFT?

The Idea

Cellule means "little cell" and is an opportunity for scientific art to navigate into the crypto world as NFTs. The founder entertained the idea for Crypto Cellules after finishing an upper-level Histology course during his undergraduate career. The idea is an embodiment of science, art, and crypto!

The Art

A Crypto Cellule collection is made up of 10,000 one-of-a-kind cellules. Right now, only one collection (columnar) will be available. Each cellule contains a base, stain, nucleus, mitochondria, granules, microvilli, cilia, and background, along with a chance for rarer traits!

The Blockchain

Crypto Cellules will be launched on OpenSea using the Ethereum blockchain. Each cellule will be priced at 0.5 Eth (.2 Eth during presale) regardless of rarity, so pay attention to those rarer traits! There is no holding for future buy-ins, so make sure to pick up yours quickly :)

But Why?

The NFT market is saturated with new assets, however, many projects do not have substance to back their ideas. Crypto Cellules aims to be the NFT homeplace to those who love and embrace science of any kind. NFT owners will eventually have access to an exclusive shared virtual room.

About

Find Out About the Backgrounds

The four background pictures used to create each Crypto Cellule are public-use photographs found on the National Cancer Institute's (NCI) visuals website. They are all photographs of real cells, most using flourescent techniques to bring out vibrant colors.

Images

  • Colon Cancer Cells

    Two of the images provided by the NCI include cancer cells from a shared/similar region of the body (colorectal : bottom right; colon: top right).

  • HeLa Cells

    HeLa Cells (bottom left) are a special type of cancer cell derived from a former cancer patient, Henrietta Lacks. HeLa cells come from one of the oldest cell lines available and also hold incredible importance for scientific research.

  • Pancreatic Cells

    The image of pancreatic cells (top left) from the NCI shows cell nuclei in blue, with membranes colored red. The pancreatic cell culture helps scientists study tumor growth factors and related cancer outcomes.

Cellule traits

Check out the Traits

Each columnar cell starts with a base, and is then built from the membrane in.

Base

One base for everyone, the most foundational and important piece of each cellule.

Background

Four different backgrounds, some rarer than others; ALL visually appealing.

Stain

Each Cellule receives one stain. 11 stains; six special, five typical. The stain colors your cellule so you can see the differentiation!

Nucleus

Four different nuclei have the chance of appearing in your cell. The centerpiece of each Crypto Cellule.

Mitochondria

The powerhouse of the cellule. No cellule would be complete without one of four mitochondria options.

Microvilli

Microvilli help increase absorption or secretion, vital to survival of each cellule. Four different options.

Cilia

Cilia help move cellular debris to keep organisms healthy. Four different options.

Granules

These granules are specific to a specialized columnar cell, four colors available.

Specialties

Specialties are rare cellular traits. If you see a specialty trait on your NFT, count yourself lucky.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will there be any more collections released?

    As of now, there are no plans to create further collections. However, if there is a demand for more Crypto Cellules in the future, we will definitely consider releasing more. If that is the case, then each cellule from the Columnar Collection will be used as a pass to get best prices on upcoming projects.

  • Will the prices of the Columnar Crypto Cellule collection change?

    No, the prices will remain static at 0.5 Eth for this collection (other than presale) while listed by the seller. They will of course change when a consumer buys and relists a Crypto Cellule. Further collections, if ever produced, will likely be set at a higher price to reward the earlier adopters. The presale price will be 0.2 Eth for 100 cellules until they sell out or we reach the actual release date.

  • How do I know which Columnar Crypto Cellules are most rare?

    The rarities of each Cellule trait are listed underneath the "Rarities" tab on the Crypto Cellules homepage. The cells with the most rare traits should "cell" at a higher price than others if choosing to resale.

  • How do I buy one?/Can I buy multiple?

    You buy the actual Crypto Cellule from the OpenSea website. There is a link above to the Crypto Cellules Columnar Collection. Also, you can buy more than one NFT, nobody is limited to just one.
